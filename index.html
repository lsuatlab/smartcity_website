About Us

Our Mission

Improve Baton Rouge and the Capitol Region using LSU research expertise and community partnerships to jointly develop and implement Smart , Safe & Connected technologies.

Approach

Addressing the region's transportation and crime issues will require a holistic approach, enabled by advanced Smart Safe & Connected (SS&C) technology. We are building a community-based partnership of regional stakeholders and researchers across disciplines. Our efforts include:

  • Engaging relevant community stakeholders,
  • Working with these partners to identify and translate infrastructure issues around transportation and crime, and
  • Utilizing advanced SS&C technologies to address these challenges.

These ongoing efforts will leverage a planned community-oriented and publicly-available web portal that will enable community stakeholders and researchers to collaborate, share information, and visualize outcomes in real-time.

Events

LSU researchers across campus are working on solving public safety and transportation challenges using Smart City methods. We invite you to join us to learn about these capabilities, and explore how we as a city and region can pursue shared Smart City initiatives.
